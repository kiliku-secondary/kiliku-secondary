School Headboy

Lessons from leadership

By Joshua Lemayian · Head Boy · June 2025

Leadership is more than just holding a title or wearing a badge—it's about responsibility, humility, and the impact we have on those around us. Recently, I have come to truly understand that being Headboy isn't about recognition or authority, but about service and example. I have learnt that real leaders listen more than they speak, lift others up, and lead by action, not just words. It changed my perspective to see leadership not as a role of privilege, but one of purpose—where every decision and behavior can inspire others and shape the school environment for the better.
