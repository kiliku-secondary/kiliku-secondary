Empowering Classrooms Through Technology: Our Journey with Camara Education

By Mr. Kelvin Wambua · Computer and ICT Teacher · June 2025

Thanks to the support of Camara Education and a shared dedication to advancing digital learning, our students are no longer just absorbing information—they’re actively creating, exploring, and innovating with it. They're not only learning how to operate technology, but also how to think critically and solve problems through it.

This transformation wouldn’t be possible without access to reliable ICT infrastructure. Camara has played a pivotal role in making that happen. Their generous donation of refurbished computers, along with practical teacher training, has revolutionized our teaching methods. Where classrooms once relied only on chalkboards, we now integrate:

  • Engaging digital lessons delivered via computers
  • Research-based assignments powered by internet access
  • Typing and coding sessions that equip learners with essential 21st-century skills

As a teacher, I’ve seen firsthand how ICT is changing the learning environment—boosting student participation, sparking curiosity, and preparing them for the demands of a technology-driven world.
