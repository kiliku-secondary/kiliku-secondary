Creating a culture of reading

By Mr. Masila Stephen · English and Literature Teacher · June 2025

Participation in structured debates plays a pivotal role in cultivating students' oral communication
and critical thinking skills. Within the debate club, the leadership places strong emphasis on meticulous preparation
and comprehensive research for each motion. This approach ensures that students engage deeply with a wide range of information sources, fostering intellectual curiosity and analytical rigor. As a result, students not only develop the ability to articulate well-reasoned arguments but also acquire interdisciplinary knowledge that extends far beyond the immediate scope of the debate topics. Such an environment contributes meaningfully to their academic and personal development.
